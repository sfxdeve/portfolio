# Craft Logbook shipped contract (showcase-heavy Case Studies)

The portfolio is a **showcase-heavy Craft Logbook**: **Home** is a short identity strip plus a dense work-index list in the first viewport (numbered rows—not cards); each **Case Study** uses a sticky/side **Capsule** meta-rail with an alternating short-text + **Showcase** body; longer bio lives on a thin **About** surface. Contact is chrome links. There is no Writing index and no dedicated contact page.

**Why this over the alternatives:** Editorial was tried and rejected. Long-form essay / interactive-notebook directions (Heckel/Comeau-style) over-weight writing. A personal app-shell (Lovin-style) is a strong navigation model but secondary to Craft Logbook tone. Dark-as-identity and purple-glow SaaS aesthetics were rejected as costume. Light neutrals, Geist Sans + Geist Mono, a low-chroma teal accent for meaning only (links, focus, interactive states—not identity washes), sparse UI motion, and artifact-first Showcases keep the site reading as developer-built. No scroll theatre; shared/view transitions are optional later, not part of the identity.

**Prototype history:** Variant B (meta-rail Case Study) won over single-column Capsule (A) and showcase-leads-each-block (C). The throwaway prototype was discarded after the verdict—do not reintroduce prototype routes as product IA.

**Scope contract:** Home, Case Studies, and thin About; contact in chrome; a small set of Case Studies (about two to three)—few and deep, not a large gallery; hire-first Capsules with peer-depth below.
