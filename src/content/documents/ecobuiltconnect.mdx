EcoBuiltConnect shipped as a marketplace that could carry a transaction beyond discovery. A buyer could evaluate limited supply, purchase across several sellers, record what was handed over, and dispute only the part that went wrong.

The judgment I carried forward is simple: when supply is finite and claims matter, responsibility has to be visible before the happy path ends—not added after the first difficult order.
